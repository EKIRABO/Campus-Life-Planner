About this Planner

A comprehensive, accessible web application designed to help university students manage their daily tasks, events, and academic commitments efficiently.

Our Mission

Campus Life Planner is built with student productivity in mind. We understand the challenges of juggling coursework, extracurricular activities, and personal commitments. This tool provides a structured, intuitive interface to help you stay organized and focused.

Key Features

Task Management

Organize tasks with titles, durations, tags, and due dates. Mark them complete as you go.

Smart Planning

View, sort, and search your records. Filter by title, duration or due dates.

Progress Tracking

Monitor your weekly activity with charts for better organization and progress tracking.

Duration Goals

Track total time spent and remaining duration against weekly targets.

Fully Accessible

Designed with ARIA labels, keyboard navigation, and screen reader support.

Manage your preferences and data.
